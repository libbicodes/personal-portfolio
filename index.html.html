Laiba Kashif — Profile

I'm Laiba — a final-year B.Sc. Information Technology student at Curtin University Dubai, working where data, product and UX meet.

I'm drawn to data analytics, BI / product analytics, UX research and user-centered digital solutions. My work connects the three: cleaning datasets, building dashboards, spotting trends, testing product flows, documenting issues and communicating insights clearly.
